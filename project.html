History of Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art with a history of over 2,000 years. It has evolved into a popular sport and fitness activity and was standardized in the 1950s and 60s. Taekwondo became an official Olympic sport in 1988 and is practiced worldwide with a focus on discipline, respect, and perseverance.
